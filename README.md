**Stranger Things**

***Genre***

- Sci-fi
- Horror
- Mystery
- Thriller
- Drama

***Plot***

A group of kids in Hawkins discovers a secret government experiment that opens a portal to another dimension called the Upside Down. As supernatural threats emerge, they fight to save their town while uncovering dark secrets and growing up together.

***Important Characters***

**Eleven (El)** – A girl with powerful psychic abilities, central to the story
**Mike Wheeler** – The loyal leader of the group
**Dustin Henderson** – Intelligent and humorous problem-solver
**Lucas Sinclair** – Brave and realistic thinker
**Will Byers** – The first victim connected to the Upside Down
**Max Mayfield** – Strong and independent
**Jim Hopper** – The town’s protective police chief
**Joyce Byers** – Will’s determined and loving mother

***Villains / Threats***

**Demogorgon** – A terrifying creature from the Upside Down
**Mind Flayer** – A powerful entity controlling the Upside Down
**Vecna/Henry/001** – A psychological and human-like main villain

***Why I Chose This Series***

I chose Stranger Things not only for its fascinating and incredible story and plot, but also because it highlights the care and love the characters have for each other. It shows strong friendship and family bonds, proving that they will always be there for one another no matter what—creating a truly inseparable connection. There are also line from the script that inspires me! *"Music has a way of finding you even in the darkest of places"* & *"I was looking for answer in somebody else but I had all the answer, I just meeded to stop being so scared"*.
